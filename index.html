About Us

Ebenezer happy Home was founded on a small Island in the Middle of Lake Victoria in Homabay County, Kenya in December 2020 to provide a haven to the growing number of orphaned and destitute children affected by the HIV/AIDS pandemic.

We provide food, shelter, education, clothing, a safe and loving environment. Our desire is to provide a holistic solution: physically, spiritually and socially; to help foster future leaders within Mfangano Island.
