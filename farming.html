Farming Project

Crop Farming

Tomatoes

On the land we produce small scale tomatoes that are sold to bring income. Some of it is in preparation of meals at the home. The children work so hard in the farm to ensure we achieve good harvest.

Kales (Sukumawiki)

Sukumawiki is a type of vegetable widely consumed in the region. Here we also grow it for consumption at home and a few of it for comercial purposes. The vegetable sales at a good rate especially when its not available in the island.

Kienyeji

Kienyeji are locally consumed vegetables. They include Spider plant, .... These type of vegetable are indeginious hence most locals consume them for their medicinal nature. We plant a bit of it in the land for comercial purposes.

Poultry Farming

Poutlry Farming

At Ebenezer Home we keep locally grown hens(Kienyeji). The hens are bassically for comercial and local consumption at the Home. The Plan is to increse the number of chik=cken in the farm and also to include different kinds of breeds of poultry.

Fish Farming

Fish Farming

Fishing is the main economic activity in the island. We embrace local fishing using simple mechanisumes such as hook and line and beach seining. These help in food production however, there is a need on venturing into modern fishing technics so as to have a good catch.
