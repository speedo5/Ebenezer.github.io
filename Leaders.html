Staff Information

Chairlady

Ruth Akinyi

I’m a pastor by God’s grace and serving the people of Mfangano Island. I’m a wife and a mother of 6 children. I’m truly blessed to wake up here every morning and just seeing how God is at work here by pouring His joy in the lives of these people. It has been a privilege working with a vibrant community who are optimistic to overcome their challenges someday.

Chief Coodinator

Cosmas Otieno

I’m a Mfangano Island native. This Island is beautiful and a mazing place to experience God’s presence from the hills, black sand beaches, sea breeze, land breeze, the busy fishing industry, amazing blue and sometimes green waters surrounding the Island. The people all reflect God’s image here. I’m a born again Christian and I love serving others first. I went through the challenging life of an orphan at a tender age (primary grade 7) and by God’s Grace a Good Samaritan from the US educated me by funding my bachelor’s degree in education science.

Treasurer/ Accountant

Roby Oguta

Mfangano Island is my home and home to many other less fortunate community due to geographical challenge of being an Island. Not many people or NGOs or even government itself visit this Island. The people are very jovial despite the economic challenges they go through. I’m a graduate accountant from African international University by God’s grace. I’m currently serving as project accountant with Redeemed gospel church development centre in partnership with compassion Kenya-Oyugis branch. As a born again believer, my passion is to serve less fortunate people through hands on projects that can empower them to be self-sustained.

Secretary/ Assistant Coordinator.
